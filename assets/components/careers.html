Senior Golang Developer

The Opportunity

The position of Senior Golang Developer provides you with an amazing opportunity to work on a project of a leading Singapore software startup. The main goal of the project is to create scalable SaaS tools and APIs used by Brands and Creators to deploy NFT collections and utilize NFTs in their brand user flow. You will be working with a talented team that possesses a wealth of knowledge and experience in building large scale, complex software systems.

What this role entails

  • Read and comprehend technical specifications, analyze product requirements
  • Collaborate with internal and oversea teams to develop software that meets a high standard of code quality, stability, maintainability, readability, and performance
  • Contribute to the architecture design/code review of the project
  • Attend meetings with international teams

What we are looking for

  • Strong Computer Science (CS) fundamentals with a Bachelor's Degree in CS (or similar technical field of study) or equivalent practical experience
  • 4+ years of experience as a web developer, including 2+ years of working with Golang
  • Good English speaking skill
  • Solid understanding and experience in Golang programming and Database
  • Ability to make technical judgements at the project level
  • Strong understanding of a project's long-term goals such as maintainability, security, performance, etc.
  • Great passion for programming
  • Willingness to learn new technologies

What we offer

  • Attractive and competitive salary
  • Project allowance based on performance
  • Annual salary review
  • 13th salary paid before Lunar New Year
  • Extra leaves and holidays
  • IT training courses and a promising career path
  • Sports and extracurricular activities
  • Friendly, enthusiastic, international working environment
  • Other good benefits

Interested candidates please send your resume to hr@fabatechnology.com.
